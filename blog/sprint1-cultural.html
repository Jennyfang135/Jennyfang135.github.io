    Share an example from your experience of where you were trying to work productively with others, but there was resistance or tension. Discuss the strategies you used at that time, how effective they were, and your reflections on what other strategies you would try now, and why.

    I worked in a call centre, quite often I needed to deal with frustrated or angry customers, when they had products/service issues, or billing issues, customers were frustrated and didn’t want to listen or accept what I explained to them even though I was trying my best to help them, that wasn’t something pleasant and a situation like that can be really frustrating, the strategies I normally tried include:

  • Listening - listen to customer’s full story first, no matter how long it is
  • show my empathy and my understanding to their issues
  • Stay calm and talk to customer slowly and in a calm and lower voice
  • Be patient, as customer wouldn’t know the products or services as much as we do, patience is very important
  • Take ownership, ensure customers that I understand their issue and I am trying my best to help them, their issues can be solved, it just takes a little time, if I can’t fix it, I will pass it onto the right person who can fix it.

    • My strategies worked most of the time, what other strategies I would try now? Using different stragies to deal with diffent cusotmer, as everyone is different, there is no way works for everyone, understanding people's needs and effective communications are the key factors of solving problems.
