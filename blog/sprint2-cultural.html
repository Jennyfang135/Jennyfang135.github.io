This learning agreement is about how I am managing my web development courses studying at EDA.

First of all, I have passion on coding/programming, I have a bachelor degree in computer science from Auckland University and programming is always my dreaming job, no doubt that I am motivated and have fully committed to this course.

My strengths are

  • I have learned Java before and have basic ideas about programming;
  • I am reponsible and well organised, once I commit to something, I will try my best to complete it;
  • I work hard and have eyes on details, I tends to finish my tasks in time and achieve my goals;
  • I am perservative and won't give up easily

My limitations are

  • I am mother of two, I have a busy life, I need to balance my study and family life, doing things efficiently is very important to me, so I must stick with my study plan and finish it on time;
  • Energy, as a mother and full time student, I have a lot things to do everyday, so it is hard to keep my energy up high at all the time, I could feel tired and frustrated at times. my plan to deal with this situations are:
    1. Have a 10 mintues study break every 2 hours, to have a cup of tea and go out to have some fresh air;
    2. Do Yoga before and after my studying as Yoga can relax my body phisically and emotionally;
    3. Practicing deep breath, it helps me to keep calm and get more oxygen for my body

How am I managing my workload in this programme?

  1. Plan well- set up a daily study plan before studying everyday and stick with it, finish my work in time, it includes my daily study and assignemnts
  2. Attend every workshop on Monday morning and weekly meeting with my facilitator Kelly;
  3. Work efficiently, stay focus and seek help when I can't work things out

  5. Doing exercise everyday, yoga, stretches or walking; eating healthy food and having a healthy life style

How and where will I see help in a timely way?

  1. stay calm, have a cup of tea, get some fresh air then try to solve the problem again;
  2. use google search
  3. still can't solve it? I will seek help from the Foundation or code-hlep-desk channel on EDA Slack
