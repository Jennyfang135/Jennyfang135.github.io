This learning agreement is about how I am managing my web development courses studying at EDA.

First of all, I have passion on coding/programming, I have a bachelor degree in computer science from Auckland University and programming is always my dreaming job, no doubt that I am motivated and have fully committed to this course.

My strengths are

  • I have learned Java before and have basic ideas about programming;
  • I am reponsible and well organised, once I commit to something, I will try my best to complete it;
  • I work hard and have eyes on details, I tend to finish my tasks in time and achieve my goals;
  • I am a good team worker, understanding and cooperative;
  • I am perservative and won't give up easily

My limitations are

  • I am mother of two, I have a busy life, I need to balance my study and family life, doing things efficiently is very important to me, so I must stick with my study plan and finish everything on time;
  • Energy, as a mother and full time student, I have a lot things to do everyday, so it is hard to keep my energy up high at all the time, I could feel tired and frustrated at times. my plan to deal with this situations are:
    1. Have a 10 mintues study break every 2 hours, to have a cup of tea and go out to have some fresh air;
    2. Do Yoga after everyday bootscamp study as Yoga can relax my body phisically and emotionally;
    3. Practicing deep breath, it helps me to keep calm and get more oxygen for my body

How am I managing my workload in this programme?

  1. Be on camp on time every day and finish what I need to do for the day; try my best not to miss a lesson
  2. Attend weekly check-in's with my Human Skills(core) facilitators;
  3. Attend End of Phase Tech-in's with my teacher;
  4. Work efficiently, stay focus and seek help when I can't work things out;
  5. Doing exercise everyday, yoga, stretches or walking; eating healthy food and having a healthy life style

How and where will I see help in a timely way?

The strategies that I will use when I am stuck:

  1. Stay calm, have a cup of tea, get some fresh air then try to solve the problem again;
  2. Use google search
  3. Still can't solve it? I will seek help from the Foundation or code-hlep-desk channel on EDA Slack
  4. Or seek help from the facilitation team

What do I expect from the facilitation team?

  • Good communication, please let me know what I need to work on, what I need to impove or focus on if there is something bring your attention; and I will report to you as well if there is some concerns raised from my side;
  • Provide advices/recommendations on my study
  • Assist me when I am stuck

I have committed to do this course and so far I have been enjoying it, I will keep it up and I am ready for the coming new challenges.
